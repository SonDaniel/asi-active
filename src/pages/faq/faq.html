FAQ General

What is ASI Active?

ASI Active is a wellness app created by ASI to encourage ASI employees to get up, get out and get more involved!

How do I get “points”?

Whenever you complete an activity or attend an event you can “check-in” for it in the correct section. For example: If you used the indoor track at the SRWC, you “check-in” the Cardio activity under the ASI Activities section for 5 points! You can even make get points for custom activities that you don’t see in the other three sections!

Why do I need my Student ID?

As ASI Active is only open to CSULB ASI employees and students, we need to ensure that the individual signing up to use the app is a current student.

What happens when I get reach a goal?

When you reach the goal, you will receive an invitation for a party to celebrate with other users who have also reached the goal!

What’s a “badge” and how do I get them?

A badge is a new easy way to track your progress and a way for ASI Active to reward you keeping consistent! You can earn badges such as the “Log-in Master” badge after you log in 3 days in a row or the “Inclusive Activist” badge after logging in Inclusive activities!

I forgot to check-in to an activity, can I still get points?

Yes! The only section that you CAN NOT do that is activities for the Event section. If you attended a one-time event, but did not check in before the event ends -- you will not be able to afterwards.

How many Custom activities can I input?

You can only input 3 custom activities per day. This means under the Custom activities section you can only earn up to 3 points.

Is there a max number of points I can achieve each day?

Yes. Each user can only earn up to 16 points per day.

How does the Leaderboard/ranking work?

At 5 a.m. every day, the Leaderboard resets and calculates the number of points each user has earned from the previous day and organizes the total number of users into rankings. Everyday your ranking may change, so keep earning those points!

Account

How do I change my Username?

To change your username: Go to Settings → Change Username

How do I change my password?

To change your password: Go to Settings → Change Password

How do I edit my profile?

You can edit your profile in 2 ways:
1. Go to Settings → Edit Profile
2. Go to Profile → Click Edit button

How do I change my profile photo?

To change your photo: Go to Settings → Edit Profile → Click the current photo → Choose new photo → Click Save
